Leebay Hotel



On a hot day we went to this place it involved a lot of climbing but once inside we could chill. We decided to work from the top and work our way down and could see that a small fire had been set here at some point as part way of the corridor was burned.

We came to the ground floor which had warnings police dogs were trained on site but overall the explore itself was flat most of the rooms looked the same.

History of Lee Bay Hotel

Closed in June 2008 the Lee Bay Hotel was originally built in the 1800's as a Manor House. Eventually this became the Lee Bay Hotel where they could accommodate up to 120 customers and employed up to 50 members of staff during the summer months. Unfortunetly we could find no information on why this place had been closed.
