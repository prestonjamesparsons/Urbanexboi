Welcome to Urbanexboi.co.uk

Come Join us as we explore across the UK from some of Britains creepiest asylums, popular night clubs to mod bases and workhouses.

We have explored over 200 locations which has seen us become one of the biggest exploring groups in the UK
Armed with only a torch and cameras we aim to preserve and document the history for future generations.

Watch our videos on YouTube using the Links. We update these regulary with new videos including tours of locations giving you a first hand view of exactly how we see things when we first enter.
If you are interested in seeing the full set of pictures from our locations please head on over to our facebook page where we keep thousands of images of our locations including some that are not on our website.
If your Interested in exploring please keep in mind that some of these buildings are highly dangerous, before we enter any building, we plan before we go in but also we carry PPE to keep us safe. We also carry out various Risk Assessments before entering a building.

Use the links to find out all the places that we have explored

We are currently not recruiting but please keep an eye on our facebook page

What is Urban Exploring?

Urban Exploring is the exploration of man-made structures, usually abandoned ruins or not usually seen components of the man-made environment. Photography and historical interest/documentation are heavily featured in the hobby and, although it may sometimes involve trespassing onto private property, this is not always the case.

Our group follow a strict code

1. Never Break in - No matter how badly we want to see a building breaking in is never the answer.


2. Never steal ... this goes against the urbexers code


3. We never put ourself into a risky situation and always ensure were safe at all times
