Tides Reach Hotel




Sitting Silenty on the beach of salcome is the Tides Reach Hotel a stones throw from the sea. We arrived outside to find that this place looked huge we entered through the pool area which is now a dumping area and squash courts. we made our way to the reception area where we found hundreds of keys behind the desk for different rooms. The Rooms all looked the same except for the pile of dead fly's in one. We spent hours in this place and there were lots to see

History of Tides Reach Hotel

Built in 1934, Closed in 2015. Art deco building has had lots of work done over the years. Quite a lot of the original fixtures and fittings are gone and the place has a bad 1990's feel to it. The bland and boring rooms are all pretty much the same, either painted magnolia or pale green. The hotel does, however, have an indoor pool, sauna, gym, squash court etc.
